Welcome to Talkomatic™!

Talkomatic is an online platform for real-time text communication, essentially a chat room. Users can type messages, and everyone in the room can see the messages as they are being typed.

Choose Your Talkomatic Experience

Talkomatic has two different versions: Classic Version and OG Version. You may choose which one you would like to visit and use below. Feel free to switch between them.

Classic Version ⇥

The actively maintained version of Talkomatic, with new features added on top of the original.

  • Open source
  • No account needed
  • Create and join chatrooms as usual
  • Draw together on a shared whiteboard in rooms
  • Play piano together in rooms
  • Themes and emoticons
  • Automod, mute, and a report system
  • Moderator dashboard with dev and mod roles
  • Leaderboard and apps
OG Version ⇥

The original version of Talkomatic that was shut down, rebuilt and preserved exactly as it was.

  • The original experience, untouched
  • Open source
  • No account needed
  • No mods or auto mod
  • No whiteboard or piano
  • For those features, use the Classic Version
